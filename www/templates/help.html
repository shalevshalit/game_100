Help
  • Press on the green dot to add a number to a square.
  • The next number must be 3 jumps to the left/right/top/bottom side and 2 jumps to a diagonal.
  • You cannot press on a square that already has number in it.
  • Revert - go to last state before last number assign.
  • Score - shows you the last number you pressed.
  • Restart - Reset the board.
  • Reach 100 to win the game.
